Bridget’s Shoe & Handbag Repair is experienced with repairs on fashion forward footwear and handbags. We take pride in offering high-quality craftsmanship and take special care of your valued items.

We offer complete shoe repair services for all types of men's and women's shoes. From replacing heels and adding new soles to fixing zippers or creating orthopedic shoes, you'll love walking in your shoes when we are finished with them. Additionally, when we repair your shoes, we always polish the shoes free of cost before we give them back to you.

Services

Quality

Shoes and purses are necessary parts of any outfit. As such, you want to make sure that they look their best at all times. It won’t do to have these essential accessories be dirty or scuffed. This is why you’ll want to check out our shoe and purse repair services.

Handbag Repair

When it comes to handbag repair, we’re true experts. We’ll be able to patch up holes in the fabric, renew colors, and more. We can even take care of leather purse restoration! You’ll be glad that you entrusted your purse to our care.

Shoe Repair

We’re also quite skilled at shoe repair. Shoes are particularly apt to suffer damage, and we know how to fix even the toughest cases. Our shoe repair shop has all of the tools and materials (not to mention expertise) that will be needed.
